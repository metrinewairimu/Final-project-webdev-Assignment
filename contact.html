Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best time to visit Kenya for safari?

The best time depends on your interests. July-October is ideal for the Great Migration in Maasai Mara, while January-March offers excellent wildlife viewing with fewer crowds and lower prices. The dry seasons (June-October and January-March) generally provide the best game viewing.

Do you offer customized safari packages?

While we don't directly operate safaris, we work with trusted local partners to help you plan customized experiences. We can connect you with reputable safari operators, recommend accommodations, and provide detailed itineraries based on your interests and budget.

What should I know about Kenya visa requirements?

Most visitors need a visa to enter Kenya. You can apply for an eVisa online before travel or get a visa on arrival. We recommend checking current requirements with the Kenyan embassy in your country, as regulations can change.

Is it safe to travel to Kenya?

Kenya is generally safe for tourists, especially in established safari areas and coastal regions. We provide up-to-date safety information and work with experienced local guides who know the areas well. As with any travel, we recommend following standard safety precautions.
